Un ordered List: =============== Contains a list which is not needed in order & gives the results in bullet points. Ex:
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Rice
Results: ======= . Milk . Eggs . Rice =========== Ordered List: ========== It organises a list numberwise. Ex:
  1. Milk
  2. Eggs
  3. Rice
Results: ====== 1.Milk 2.Eggs 3.Rice
